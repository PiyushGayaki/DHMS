Core Features

Everything a Clinic Needs

Built for real-world clinical workflows — from patient registration to final billing, nothing is left out.

👤
Patient Management
Register patients with full profile — name, age, gender, phone, village. Instant search and visit history lookup.
🩺
Visit & Vitals Tracking
Record weight, temperature, SpO₂, blood pressure and pulse per visit. Track visit status across Created → Prescribed → Closed.
💊
Smart Prescriptions
Prescribe medicines with dosage (morning/afternoon/evening/night), units, food instructions and dose-per-time. Save as reusable medicine protocols.
💉
IV & Injection Protocols
Create saline + injection combinations with Mix-in-Saline or Push modes. Manage free vs. charged items. Bundle into named reusable protocols.
🧾
Integrated Billing
Auto-generate bills from prescriptions and procedures. Track doctor fees, medicine totals, due amounts, partial payments and due recovery.
📦
Inventory & Batch Management
Maintain medicines, salines and injections with batch-level expiry tracking. DB triggers auto-update stock quantities on every batch change.
📊
Reports & Audit Logs
Daily reports, low-stock alerts, cash logs, billing search and deep audit trails. Know the state of your clinic at a glance.
📝
Note Templates & History
Save doctor note templates and medical history items for fast re-use. Track patient symptoms and advice per visit.
💵
Cash Register
Frontdesk cash log for ADD/SPEND entries with daily balance tracking. Transparent financial oversight for every shift.

Patient Journey

Seamless End-to-End Workflow

1

Patient Registration

Frontdesk registers the patient with personal details and creates a visit, recording up-front vitals.

2

Doctor Consultation

Doctor reviews visit queue, examines patient, documents symptoms, diagnosis and notes with reusable templates.

3

Prescription & Protocols

Doctor prescribes medicines and IV procedures using saved protocols or custom entries, then closes the visit.

4

Medicine Dispensing

Medical store fulfills the prescription — stock is automatically deducted from confirmed batch quantities.

5

Billing & Payment

Frontdesk generates the bill, collects payment (full or partial), tracks dues and updates the cash register.

6

Reporting & Audit

Admin reviews daily revenue reports, stock levels, audit logs and cash balance for full financial visibility.

Why DHMS?

Built for Real Clinics

🔌

100% Offline Operation

Runs on a local MariaDB instance — no internet required. Full clinic operation even with zero connectivity. Perfect for rural and semi-urban clinics.

🖥️

One-Click Desktop App

Packaged as two native Windows executables (Server + Client) with PyInstaller. No Python or setup required — just double-click and you're live.

🔒

Secure by Design

CSRF protection on every form, bcrypt password hashing, session fixation prevention, and role-based access control baked in at the framework level.

⚡

Production-Grade Server

Powered by Waitress WSGI server with 8 worker threads — handles concurrent clinic staff with ease. Enterprise reliability without enterprise cost.

🔄

Smart DB Triggers

MariaDB triggers automatically sync stock quantities on every batch insert, update or delete — zero manual reconciliation needed.

📋

Printable Prescriptions

Auto-generated, print-ready prescription layouts for patients. Clean, professional output with medicine dosage, instructions and doctor details.
